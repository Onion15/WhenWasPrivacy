Privacy Policy for WhenWas

Last updated: June 12, 2024

Introduction

Thank you for using WhenWas! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains the types of information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

User Data:

  • Interface preferences such as font size, primary color, and theme mode.
  • Event information entered by you including event names, dates, and descriptions.

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Technical information about the device, including the operating system version and screen resolution, may be collected to optimize app performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Customize the user interface according to your preferences.
  • Provide functionality related to scheduling and managing events.
  • Improve and develop new features for the app.

How We Store and Protect Your Data

Your data is stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. We implement security measures such as encryption to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of data no longer necessary for the purposes for which they were collected.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page.
